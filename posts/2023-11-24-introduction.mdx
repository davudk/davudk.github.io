So to introduce myself: my name is Davud.

I develop software for a living. As a kid I loved video games.
My interest in games eventually led to an interest in computers and how they work.
In an effort to figure things out, I probably broke more computers and electronics as a kid than most people ever had.

(In my defense, I broke stuff for educational purposes.)

Anyways, I setup this blog so I'd have a place to share my thoughts about software and technology, as well as my thoughts about life.

I put together this site over the last couple days.
I initially wanted to set it up on [Ghost](https://ghost.org/), but the pricing is high.
Wordpress is more affordable, but I eventually settled on GitHub pages, which has no fees.

I guess I'll post here once in a while.
